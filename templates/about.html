About Us

Legacy Garden helps youth and seniors build meaningful connections in a world where seniors often feel lonely and youth may not get to learn from older generations.We believe every shared story strengthens understanding. This platform offers a warm space for both generations to connect and grow together.

Our Mission

To build a safe and joyful online space where youth and seniors can share stories, chat, play activities together, and watch their digital garden grow with every interaction.

What We Built

Legacy Garden is simple and friendly to use. Every small action helps your garden bloom.

🌸 Share Stories
Write about your day or upload a photo. Each story gives you water droplets to grow plants in your personal garden.
🎮 Play Activities Together
Youth and seniors can join fun mini-games and challenges. Completing these activities earns seeds for growing flowers and trees.
💬 Chat and Build Streaks
Send messages regularly. Talking every day builds a streak, which rewards you with more seeds.
🌱 Grow Your Garden
Use your seeds and water to grow new plants. When your flowers or trees are fully grown, you can harvest them for points.
🌳 Join Your Community
Everyone in your region shares one big Community Tree. Your harvested plants help this tree grow and bloom. You can also see region updates, top contributors, and community achievements.

Why Legacy Garden Matters

Legacy Garden helps people of different ages connect through small, meaningful moments. Whether it’s sharing a story, completing a mission together, or chatting daily, these interactions help build a strong, caring community.

Our goal is to make every young user or pioneer user to feel valued, heard, and connected.
