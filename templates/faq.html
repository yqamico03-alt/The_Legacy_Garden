Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Legacy Garden?
Legacy Garden is a warm and simple website that helps youth and seniors connect through stories, chat, and digital garden growth with every interaction.
2. How do I earn seeds and water droplets?
Seeds come from playing activities and keeping messaging streaks. Water comes from posting stories. Both are used to grow plants in your personal garden.
3. How do activities work?
Youth and a senior complete a mini-game together. When both finish, you receive seeds to help your garden grow.
4. What is the Community Tree?
Each region in Singapore has one shared tree. When members harvest flowers or trees from their personal garden, the region’s Community Tree grows too.
5. What is a messaging streak?
If you and your partner message each other daily, your streak increases. Longer streaks give more bonus seeds.
6. Is this website safe for seniors?
Yes. We use guidelines and supportive features to create a safe space.
7. What happens when I harvest a plant?
You earn points, and your region’s Community Tree grows. Harvesting shows you have completed growing a meaningful moment.
8. Do I need a partner?
You can use the website alone, but activities and messaging are more meaningful with a youth or senior partner.
9. Why is my plant growing slowly?
Plants grow when you earn more water (stories) and seeds (activities or messaging). Just keep interacting, and growth will increase.
10. Do I need to use my real name or photo?
No. You may choose what you feel comfortable sharing. A nickname or profile icon is fine.
11. What if I forget how to use something?
You can always return to the About Us page for a guide.
